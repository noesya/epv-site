BALDET, a staircase manufacturer, is based in Durenque, a small village in Aveyron 30 km south of Rodez. Today, more than 2500 m2 of modern premises house this staircase factory in the heart of Lévézou. Three generations have succeeded each other at the head of the carpentry shop, always with the same passion for wood and beautiful workmanship. Today, the team is made up of 15 employees, all qualified or in training. In a logic of quality, each skilled worker is responsible for his staircase from production to installation, helped by a young worker or an apprentice. In addition to the 10 types of wood on offer, the company uses various materials such as steel, aluminum, glass or stainless steel.
