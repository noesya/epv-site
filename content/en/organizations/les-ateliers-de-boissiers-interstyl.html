A family business for three generations, Interstyl designs, creates and produces high-end knitwear for the luxury apparel market. All these products are manufactured exclusively on its site in France.

Founded in Paris in 1966, Interstyl moved to Auvergne in 1972, a few kilometers from Le Puy en Velay.
