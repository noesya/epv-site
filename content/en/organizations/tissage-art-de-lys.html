Since 1890, Tissage Art de Lys has been creating, weaving and making collections of high-end jacquard patterns to decorate the interiors of today and tomorrow. The workshops are located in Lys-Lez-Lannoy, in the heart of the historic textile basin of Northern France.

Tissage Art de Lys is the guarantor of exceptional know-how inherited from the Middle Ages and unanimously recognized in France and internationally. A regular partnership with the RMNGP (Réunion des Musées Nationaux et Grand Palais) demonstrates high quality manufacturing. In 2013, the Tissage Art de Lys company was awarded the EPV (Living Heritage Company) label. The EPV label is awarded by the Ministry of the Economy, Industry and Employment, and is the only State distinction associated with the recognition of exceptional know-how.
