Ressource is an independent, family-owned paint and wallpaper publishing house based in Provence since 1946. Heir to artisanal know-how resulting from the work of ocher quarries, Ressource has always had as its driving force the desire to undertake sustainably. The place of ecology is at the heart of its development, all paints and wallpapers, without exception, are made in France in the Maison's workshops, favoring sustainable manufacturing. The spirit of Ressource is illustrated by abundant creativity combined with a capacity for permanent innovation. Each of the shades are designed by its in-house creation studio to offer exceptional shade quality.
