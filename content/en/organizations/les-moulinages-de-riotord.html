"Les Moulinages de Riotord is a French textile and silk company based in Lyon, with two plants in the Rhône Alpes Auvergne region. Specializing in exceptional products, our markets are naturally focused on luxury and high-end goods. In this increasingly volatile and complex world, we represent the values of professionalism, entrepreneurial spirit and fighting spirit, as well as our attachment to history and know-how. Discover the world of the Textile and Silk Industry, a unique know-how. You can take advantage of our career opportunities, and learn a trade or practice your own. We want to attract the younger generation who work out of conviction, not duty.
