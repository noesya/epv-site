A skilful blend of artisan know-how and industrial excellenceVERRE LABO MULA combines the artisan know-how of glass and quartz blowers, handed down from generation to generation for over 60 years, with industrial excellence. Its customers include leading-edge industries in the fields of chemistry, biology, pharmaceuticals, cosmetics and agri-food, as well as engineering school and university laboratories.
