The Combes house was founded in 1923. It is above all a family story deeply attached to the values ​​of its terroir and that of the manual production of Roquefort. Thus for three generations, the Combes family has brought its particular sensitivity to the development of Vieux Berger, drawing inspiration from simple and immutable principles.
