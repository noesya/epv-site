Since 1947, at Ferme des Peupliers, we've been processing our cows' milk exclusively, to bring you a wide range of yogurts, milk and fromage frais. Our tasty products regularly win medals at the Concours Général Agricole.

Our dairy know-how has been passed on with passion for 4 generations. We are proud to have them recognized by the French government as part of France's heritage. We innovate to preserve them, combining tradition and modernity.

We are two brothers committed, with our teams, to perpetuating the model we believe in. That's why we continually invest to improve and ensure consistent product quality. Concerned about environmental issues, we are determined to reduce our impact on the planet.
