Our workshop is located in Gouville-sur-Mer (50), in Normandy, France.SAS Ferronnerie PICARD DUBOSCQ designs custom-made creations, restorations and copies of antiques, according to the rules of the art.Our experienced journeymen ironworkers and welders have acquired ancient techniques. We work and visit both professional and private customers. Visits to our company are available on request.
