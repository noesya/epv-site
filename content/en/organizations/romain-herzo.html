 Romain Herzo is first and foremost a beautiful story. Eric Herzo has been passionate about jewelry since childhood, and his creations are inspired by his multicultural environment and faraway lands. After years in the profession, Eric Herzo decided to open his own jewelry workshop in Lyon in 1990, and in 1992 the house of Romain Herzo was born. Decades of experience, boundless creativity and exceptional craftsmanship are the strength of the company.

The designer is constantly on the lookout for originality and perfection. A subtle blend of expert craftsmanship and modernity, his creations are timeless. When a jeweler's creativity meets a woman's desires, Romain Herzo's talent gives life to unique, personalized pieces that burst with emotion and eternal promise.
