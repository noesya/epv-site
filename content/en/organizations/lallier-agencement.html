Wood, metal and glass fittings for the luxury sector

Specializing in woodworking for interior architecture, decoration and design since 1888, LALLIER has never ceased to evolve, adapting to new techniques and materials without ever forgetting the fundamentals of traditional French joinery and cabinetmaking.

Located in Hauterive, near Valence, the company's 40 joiners, cabinetmakers and lacquerers have always worked with the same attention to detail, attentiveness and excellence.

In 2003, LALLIER acquired a fine locksmith company based in Annecy.

With its two traditional skills (wood and metal) now indispensable in high-end interior design, LALLIER has always been able to meet the increasingly demanding and complex requirements of its prestigious customers in France and abroad.
