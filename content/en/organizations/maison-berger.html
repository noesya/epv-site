MAISON BERGER PARIS is a leading heritage brand in the home fragrance market. Founded in 1898, Maison Berger is the originator of the iconic Berger lamp and its patented technology: a ceramic burner that purifies and perfumes the air.

Since then, the group has diversified its product offering, proposing a complete range of products to decorate and beautify indoor air.
