With 30 years' experience in the industry, Lucal is a recognized expert in the eyewear production cycle from A to Z, from design to delivery of the final product. Based in Oyonnax in the French Jura region, Lucal stands out for its highly skilled workforce, including several "Meilleurs Ouvriers de France". For this experienced team, the highest standards of quality are second nature.
