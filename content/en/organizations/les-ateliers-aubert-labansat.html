Known and recognized for our expertise, we specialize in the restoration of historic monuments (castles, manor houses, abbeys, cathedrals, churches, etc.) and ancient heritage for carpentry and joinery work. We restore, reproduce identically, or create new works respecting the aesthetics of the old. We also carry out trellis decorations and special woodwork.

Based in Coutances, our workshop and site teams, trained over the years since 1890, work mainly in north-west France.
