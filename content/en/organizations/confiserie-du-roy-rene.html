Founded in 1920, Le Roy René is a family-run business based in Aix-en-Provence, specializing in calissons, nougats and other Provencal sweets. Now a hundred years old, the company's expertise has been handed down from generation to generation. Artisan confectioners select exceptional raw materials, and repeat the same gestures acquired from their predecessors, with respect for tradition.
