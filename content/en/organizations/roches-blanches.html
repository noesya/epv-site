Family-owned for three generations, we are proud to maintain the quality and excellence that characterize our products. Roches Blanches is dedicated exclusively to the artisanal production of dry sausages.

Our ambition is to perpetuate this craft with authenticity, respecting today's standards while honoring the legacy of our ancestor, Marcel Malandain. With a motivated team, we have been able to evolve as an innovative artisanal company, remaining faithful to our traditions.
