meta_description: >- summary: >-

Created in 2021, the association deploys its action in favor of the know-how of the Southern region. Its mission is to federate, represent and promote companies in the South of France with the EPV label.

header_text: >- header_cta: display: false label: >- target: "" external: false contents_reading_time: seconds: 62 text: >- 1 minute contents: - kind: heading title: >- Missions slug: "missions" position: 1 rank: 2 - kind: block template: chapter title: >- ranks: self: false data: layout: no_background text: >-
  • Create a regional network in liaison with the existing national network of companies awarded the "Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant" (EPV) label by the French government.
  • Participate in the actions of regional associations pursuing the same goals, contributing to the expression of the needs/difficulties of companies in terms of recruitment and training, and to the implementation of the most appropriate solutions.
  • Contribute to initiatives aimed at safeguarding, preserving and passing on the region's rare artisanal and industrial skills of excellence.
  • Enlist the support of all industrial and craft companies in the region with rare and exceptional know-how.
  • Organize actions for the benefit of certified companies within our region, including meetings, information exchanges, sponsorship, and sales promotion and communication initiatives.
  • Set up partnerships with professional organizations, public organizations, local authorities, training bodies, economic sectors (financial partners in particular) and the media to support the development of EPVs in the region.
  • Promote the EPV (Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant) label in the region, in France and internationally.
  • Promote EPV companies to investment funds and banks in order to obtain equity and quasi-equity financing.
