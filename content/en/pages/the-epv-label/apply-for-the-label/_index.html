Would you like to join the EPV Excellence Network? To benefit from the many programs and advantages on offer, you must first apply for the label.

header_text: >- header_cta: display: false label: >- target: "" external: false contents_reading_time: seconds: 26 text: >- less than a minute contents: - kind: block template: chapter title: >- ranks: self: false data: layout: no_background text: >-

Support for economic development, tax incentives, promotion of the label... In addition to government recognition of their know-how and role, Entreprises du Patrimoine Vivant (Living Heritage Companies) can benefit from a host of advantages. But are you eligible?

notes: >- alt: >- credit: >- - kind: block template: chapter title: >- THE CRITERIA ranks: self: 2 data: layout: no_background text: >-

Companies awarded the "Entreprises du Patrimoine Vivant" label are characterized by :

  • the possession of a specific economic heritage stemming from manufacturing experience,
  • the application of complex and/or rare know-how based on the mastery of highly technical and/or traditional techniques,
  • attachment to a region and a reputation in France and/or abroad.
notes: >-

To find out more, visit the Institut pour les Savoir-Faire Français website.
